Swaplet Seas

Join us in the Swaplet Seas tutoring to make your journey of studying math alot more adventurous and fun.

We believe everyone can do well as long as they enjoy what they are learning and try hard enough.

Our approach is to make studying fun, interactive and rewarding, so students can engage in practicing and learning

We have a range of ways to promote studying.

This includes but are not limited to:

  • Interactive Tutoring Session

    We do realize we learn more by doing stuff rather then being spoon fed one hour of lesson stream each week. we will make our lessons interactive and engaging as we could get.

  • Game System

    The main way to learn maths effectively is to practice. To make practicing less dull and more engaging, we will have mini math games during sessions and long games to encourage practicing.

  • Rewards and Prices

    Positive Reinforcement is a good way to encourage practicing. We will use a part of our income to make or buy prices for our students prices will be a part of award of our game.

  • Group Working

    Being alone is very tough. Especially when it comes to doing revision. It requires a high amount of discipline. We encourage students to work together as a team to support each other in their journey and thrive the best.

  • Moral Support

    We've been there before and understand HSC journey is not some easy task we can breeze through. We can offer help for whoever feeling discourage despite trying very hard.
